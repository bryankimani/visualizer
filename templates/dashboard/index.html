Welcome to Visualizer

Visualizer is a web application that analyses online social media by mining data from social media. It analyses the sentiment how people feel about different political parties or politicians by analysing their sentiments on social media Facebook and Twitter respectively. The main aim of the app is to investigate how peoples’ feelings towards different political parties change over time after and before a major event such as Brexit or an election. It utilizes a data mining algorithm by searching for key words in people’s posts and then analyses them to produce trends using an algorithm to average out the results similar to google trends where people see the differences between each party and sentiment.
